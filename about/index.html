What we believe

Technology is only as trustworthy as the people who build it. These three principles decide how we work, who we hire, and what we ship.

Commitment

Follow-throughDedicationPerseveranceReliability

The work isn’t done at the demo or the award. It’s done when it ships and holds up under real conditions. We stay embedded until the mission is in production, see hard problems all the way through, and stand behind what we deliver.

Unity

SolidarityShared PurposeCollaborationAlignment

The mission is bigger than any one of us. Align diverse strengths around a common goal and the whole becomes greater than the sum of its parts. Debate openly, decide together, then move forward as one. Pulling in the same direction is how the hardest problems get solved.

Agency

Self-determinationChoiceOwnershipFinal Say

People have the final say. Individuals own their work and are accountable for it, with the room to exercise real control over their decisions. Leaders are there to provide counsel, not commands. The person at the helm stays empowered by many perspectives while their own talent, experience, and judgment carry the day.

What we do

SMART stands for Secure Mission Accelerated Readiness Technology. We ship products and integrate frontier security into the platforms our customers already run.

SMART Workspace

Browser-accessible, kernel-isolated Linux dev environments, deployable on infrastructure you own, with a tamper-evident, signed audit trail. See the platform.

Secure cloud platforms

Cloud-native platforms engineered and operated on Kubernetes, with DevSecOps pipelines that carry code from commit to production while producing continuous compliance evidence.

Post-quantum defense

We lead product development and integration of QryptoTrust’s ZAP, automating post-quantum certificate generation, verification, distribution, and compliance into one zero-trust workflow.
