The Mission

We want highly motivated, committed leaders who will put frontier-quality post-quantum cryptography on any data plane to defend the United States and her allies. AI-enhanced cyberwarfare is here today. Adversaries are already running harvest-now-decrypt-later attacks, storing encrypted data to break on Q-day, the arrival of a cryptographically relevant quantum computer. That date keeps shifting earlier, and we are building the defense that has to be ready first.

76DEV leads product development, design, and integration of QryptoTrust’s ZAP (Zero Trust AI Managed PQC), an agent that automates post-quantum certificate generation, verification, distribution, and compliance auditing into one zero-trust workflow. ZAP uses NIST PQC (Kyber and Dilithium) and runs with a Zero Trust Policy Server in the Qrypto Exchange solution, which powers secure data transfer, overlay networking, and distributed compute for government and commercial customers.
